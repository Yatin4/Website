IIT Kharagpur Research Internship

Underwent a research internship in the field of Robot-based manufacturing. The aim was to automate the workflow of a typical CNC machine in a way that the sequential operations are not user-dependent. It is also envisioned to pave way for robotic applications and simulations using RoboDK software. Automating the sequential operations of converting the orthographic views to obtaining the G-codes, and eventually to Robot language was also thought of as an extension to the project.
