Energy Backpack

It is a backpack that utilizes human limbs’ kinetic energy and stores it in a battery for later usage, specifically advantageous for trekkers. A conceptual design of the product was made using SOLIDWORKS to explain its working mechanism with the help of animations and motion study. The swinging motion of leg and hands are used to pull the strings, which is used to rotate a system of pulley and gears, which in turn rotates the shaft of the AC generator producing electricity.
