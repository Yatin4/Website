One-Sweep

One-Sweep is a solution to the existing problem of blackboard dusters not being able to clean the board efficiently. This mechanism (manually operable) achieves the cleaning of the entire board with a single sweep across the length of the board.This blackboard duster works on the principle of a pantograph mechanism. It can be put in simple terms as a translational motion scissor hinge system. A simple horizontal to vertical linear motion converting mechanism is used here to reduce the difficulty in using the machine. It is achieved with the help of a diagonal rod constrained by its length at is two ends. These features make this machine unique and efficient.
